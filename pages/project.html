MLWS precision website

MLWS precision mlws was a company i made an internship at, during this time i was assigned to redesign the web page they used to have.

Based on the project parameters in this case, due to the short time to create the project, these phases were a bit different. mlws precision is a mechanical precision company specialized in the design, creation and tech of the medical industry. the website purpose is to show what they are capable to do and what services can they bring to external companies.

The website is optimized to guide users into what’s more important for them specifically. The final product was built after several usability tests to assure the website was easy to use and understand.
